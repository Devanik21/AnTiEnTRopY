$H(\beta) = -\beta \log_2(\beta) - (1-\beta) \log_2(1-\beta)$

$E_c(\mathbf{q}) = \sum_{i=1}^{k} \exp(-\gamma\|\mathbf{q} - \mathbf{x}^{(c)}_i\|_2^2) \cdot (1 + \cos(\omega_c\|\mathbf{q} - \mathbf{x}^{(c)}_i\|_2))$

$\Delta a_{\text{net}}(p, T) = T - R(p) \le 0$
